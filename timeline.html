Mouse Gestures

  • HoverHighlight a commit and show details.
  • ClickLock the selected commit.
  • Click empty / EscClear locked commit.
  • Left-dragPan the timeline view.
  • WheelZoom in/out on the timeline.
  • Ctrl + drag / wheelScroll through groups.
  • Left Pan: CombinedLeft-drag also scrolls groups.

Hotkeys

  • FToggle fullscreen mode.
  • ← / →Previous/next commit.
  • ↑ / ↓Move between groups.
  • Home / EndFirst/last commit in group.
  • EscClear locked commit.
